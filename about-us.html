About Modern Education World School

MODERN owes its existence to the vision of eminent educationist and philanthropist Mr. POKAR RAM POONIA whose foresight was revolutionary, quest for excellence was persistent, thirst for knowledge was insatiable and a passion for growth was limitless. Modern Prince envisions an education system that breeds inspiration and innovation, it kindles the dormant forces, faculties and talents of the students. We provide a platform for children where they can shed off their inhibitions and turn into confident citizens for the future.

Modern Education World School (an integrated unit of MODERN PRINCE SCHOOL SANSTHYAN) was started in the year 1987 by Sh. Pokar Ram Poonia With their strong ditermination and with the help of faithful teachers. Its started as a primary school but today with their efforts this intitution has progress as Senior Secondary School.

Its offering Science, Art, Commerce. With their rainful efforts the school also started English Medium till 10th standard. The English Medium School is situated on Taranagar-Churu road. The motto of the school is 'Kids fly free'. The school has built up a tradition which incorporates in its milieu all that is universally good in thought, word and deed. The faculty of Modern Prince consists of number of highly qualified teachers who have extensive teaching experience in their respective fields.

The aim of this website is to convey as far as possible the essence of Modern Education World School, which pursues an academic procedure with a view to ensure excellence in scholastic and co-scholastic areas. A sincere attempt has been made within the limited scope of pages of this website to furnish you with as much information possible concerning the academic entity and enable you to take one of the most crucial decisions about the academic career of your child, the impact of which would be nothing less than the destiny in an unknown future. The opportunities offered by us will provide your child with multidimensional growth making him an amiable and affable.

Main Features:
