Modern Prince

Welcome

In this competitive world Modern Education World School is the first choice of entire Taranagar region because it is a unique, different and one of the best institutions of Rajasthan equipped with modern facilities. We assure you that our school has developed a process by which knowledge character and behavior of our students are shaped and moulded. We instil the feeling of confidence and competition among our students. The school is dedicated to prepare conscientious and responsible citizens with a sense of integrity and honesty who will be imbibed with a spirit of adventure exploration research and creation. The school believes in the worth, uniqueness and fostering each individuals potentialities for successful learning and growth. The school provides tremendous opportunity to make all round development of each and every individual aspirate to show his/her profound talent, culture and skill in the games and sports, computers and literary activities. We warmly welcome to all the students in the area of education for the brightest future.
