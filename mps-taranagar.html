Scheme of Examination:

For Classes Nursery to II-

There will be one written Annual exam and Assessment will be done on the basis of class assignments.

For Classes III to IX & XI-

The academic year will be divided into five term:

Term Type of Term Duration of Term % of Weightage
First Test Written Aug-20 to Aug-25 10%
Second Test Written Oct-25 to Nov-05 10%
Half-Yearly Written & Oral Dec-10 to Dec-25 30%
Third Test Written Feb-15 to Feb-20 10%
Yearly/Annual Written & Oral Mar-05 to Apr-30 40%
TOTAL 100%
For Classes X & XII-
  • The academic year will be divided into two terms. In the first term there will be two unit test of 25 marks & each of 1 hour duration each & Half Yearly examination of 100 marks.
  • In the Second term there will be one Pre-Board examinations of 100 marks each.
  • Annual Exam is conduct by RBSE Board.

Result & News

Sports:

At MEWS, we believe in the philosophy- a sound mind in a sound body encourages students to be a part of sports activities. Professional coaches and trainers are being engaged with a view to have an active sporting atmosphere in the beautifully landscaped eco-friendly campus. Track and field facilities are also being envisaged to hold inter-school and national competitions in future.


At the campus level facilities for:

  • Cricket
  • Volleyball
  • Basket Ball
  • KhoKho
  • Kabaddi
  • Badminton
  • Indoor Games

Farewell:

The glittering evening at MEWS when Class 10 is bid farewell is another day which is much awaited for. The outgoing students are duly invited in prescribed dress codes to attend the function held in their honor. The head boy/girl is usually given the platform to express the mixed feelings of achievement and separation which is always the highlight of the event. Some spice is added to give special flavor to the dance party by awarding students for various specifications.


Awards at the farewell-

  • Mr. MEWS
  • Miss. MEWS
  • Best Dressed (Male/Female)
  • Best Dancer (Male/Female)

Annual Funcation:

Annual Function of any school is the culmination point of all school activities. It is the session’s round up where all achievements are shared with parents from a grand stage. Annual Function at Modern Prince is one of the gala events attracting not only its students but media as well as the city’s attention due to the heights of grandeur and magnificence attained by the cultural performances in this well timed and finely managed event.

Annual Awards-

The Annual Awards are the most important segment of the celebrations. These felicitations are the most coveted and prestigious for the young aspiring students. They are the best way of recognizing and bestowing praise and accolade to the deserving talents.

Criteria for Annual Awards-

The applicants have to be outstanding in their fields for which they apply viz – sports, cultural and academics. On the basis of prospective achiever’s presentation and an over view of the Annual Award committee, the awardees are decided on merit criteria.

Educational Tour:

An educational tour is a part of the academic experience in most schools. Such tours help students to understand firsthand about various subjects.

For instance, schools may take students on educational tours to zoos and parks to acquaint them with flora and fauna. It is one thing to see the picture of an animal or a plant in a book and quite another to see it face to face.
